My Letter to You

My dearest Jupiter,


I know today feels heavy. I know your body is tired and the world feels a bit too loud right now. If I were there, I’d be holding your hand, bringing you exactly what you need, and making sure you didn't have to lift a finger.


You are my "psycho"—the one who keeps my life exciting—and my "Jupiter," the massive, beautiful center of my gravity. Even from a thousand miles away, my heart is sitting right there beside you on the couch.


Please rest for me. Drink plenty of water and let yourself just *be*. You don't have to be strong today. I'm strong enough for both of us.


Yours, entirely ♡
